Questions?

Does this replace my POS?

No. BevAlc OS works alongside Toast, Square, Arryved, Commerce7, or whatever you're running. We're not trying to replace your POS‚Äîwe turn transactions into follow-up. Your existing systems stay exactly where they are.

We're migrating from WineDirect to Commerce7. Will this make it worse?

No. BevAlc OS sits on top of your tech stack. We connect to whatever you're using now and whatever you migrate to. One less thing to worry about during the transition.

Is this another "all-in-one" tool that does everything badly?

No. We do six things: missed call recovery, booking, reminders, reviews, reactivation, and private event follow-up. That's it. We don't touch your POS, your club management, or your inventory. We just make sure guests come back.

What about SMS compliance?

All SMS is 10DLC registered and TCPA compliant. We handle opt-in language, STOP/HELP responses, and frequency disclosure during setup. We only message guests who opt in. Your compliance officer will be bored.

How much time does setup take me?

About 30 minutes of your time. You fill out one onboarding form with your business details, hours, and branding. We build everything else. You get a Loom walkthrough when it's ready. Most venues go live within a week.

What if I already use Tock or another booking tool?

You can keep using Tock for reservations if you want. BevAlc OS can work alongside it‚Äîwe'd handle the follow-up automation (reminders, reviews, reactivation). Or you can use our booking calendar and stop paying marketplace fees on your own traffic. Your call.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. If it's not working for you, cancel with one email. We'll help you export your data.
