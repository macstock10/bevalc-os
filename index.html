Get Started

Questions? Answers below.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

No. We handle all the setup. You get a simple dashboard to see what's happening, but the system runs itself. If you can send a text, you can use BevAlc OS.

What if I already use Toast, Square, or Eventbrite?

BevAlc OS works alongside your existing tools and does not replace your POS or ticketing. You get a dedicated booking calendar you can embed on your website or link to directly. We connect your Google Business Profile for reviews. Your existing systems stay exactly as they are.

How much time does this take me each week?

Plan on checking your shared inbox once or twice a day to respond to conversations that need a human touch. We provide reply templates for common questions. Everything else runs automatically in the background, including reminders, review requests, and reactivation.

What are the usage fees?

SMS and phone usage are billed at cost to your card, separate from your subscription. Most venues see somewhere in the range of $20 to $50 per month depending on volume. During onboarding, we set up a dedicated phone number for your venue and configure your email sending. Billing is transparent and you can see usage anytime.

How does SMS compliance work?

All SMS messaging is fully compliant. We only send messages to guests who opt in, either when they book, inquire, or fill out a form on your site. Every message includes STOP to unsubscribe and HELP for support. We handle the compliance setup during onboarding, including adding opt-in language to your booking and inquiry forms.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts and no cancellation fees. If it's not working for you, cancel with one email. We will help you export your data.

What type of venues is this for?

BevAlc OS is built specifically for craft beverage venues, including distilleries, breweries, wineries, tasting rooms, and whiskey bars. If you host tastings, tours, or events, this is for you.
